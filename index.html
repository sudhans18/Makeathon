What is Makeathon 7.0?

Makeathon 7.0 is a premier 24-hour hardware and software creation sprint. We gather the brightest minds—from novices taking a leap of faith to seasoned veterans—to push the boundaries of technology. Here, innovation knows no dimensions.

Assemble your crew, pick your challenge, and collaborate. You will gain access to cutting-edge tools, elite mentorship, and a community of creators. "That person who helps others simply because it should or must be done... is undoubtedly a real superhero."
