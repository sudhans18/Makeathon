MAKEATHON 7.0 TIMELINE

🚀 25th March 2026

Problem Statements Release, Website Launch & Registration Opens

The journey begins! Problem statements go live, the official website launches, and registrations open for all teams.

Registration & PPT Idea Submission Deadline

Last day to register your team and submit your PPT idea presentation. Don't miss the cutoff!

📢 7th April 2026

Announcement of Shortlisted Finalists & Waiting List Teams

Shortlisted finalists and waiting list teams are announced. Payment registration opens for confirmed participants.

💳 10th April 2026

Payment Registration Ends

Payment registration closes. RSVP will be sent to all confirmed finalists with event details and logistics.

Make-A-Thon 7.0 Kickoff & Inauguration Ceremony

The 24-hour National Level Hackathon officially begins! Join us for the grand inauguration ceremony and kickoff.

Make-A-Thon 7.0 wraps up with the announcement of winners and the valedictory ceremony. Celebrate the innovation!
