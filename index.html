Mission Briefing

LOG ENTRY 01

What is Makeathon 7.0?

Makeathon 7.0 is an epic 72-hour creation marathon that brings together the brightest minds in technology, design, and science. Teams compete to design and prototype innovative Symbiont solutions — bio-inspired, AI-nanite hybrid concepts that push boundaries across real-world domains.

LOG ENTRY 02

The Symbiont Concept

Symbionts are living-tech hybrids — imagine neural networks grown from organic substrates, nanite swarms that self-organize to solve complex problems, or bio-engineered drones that adapt to their environment. Your creations will blur the line between biology and technology.

LOG ENTRY 03

Why Participate?

Push the boundaries of innovation across 8+ specialized biome categories. Win prizes, gain mentorship from industry leaders, and join a community of creators building the living future. No idea is too ambitious — this is where impossible becomes prototype.

LOG ENTRY 04

Categories & Themes

From AI neural jungles to blockchain crystal caves, green-tech wind forests to medtech bio-domes — each biome represents a challenge domain. Choose your terrain, assemble your crew, and build something the world has never seen.
